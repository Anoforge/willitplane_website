Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This page explains what Will It Plane collects, why, and how you can opt out.

Analytics (Google Analytics 4)

If you click "Accept" on the cookie banner, we load Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors find and use this site — for example, which pages are viewed and roughly how much traffic the site gets. This may involve a cookie set by Google in your browser. We do not sell this data or use it for advertising, and we don't receive your name, email, or other directly identifying information through it.

Google's own handling of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

If you click "Decline," or don't interact with the banner, Google Analytics is never loaded and no analytics cookie is set.

Change your choice

You can update your analytics choice at any time.

Voting & object suggestions

The "Object of the week" vote and the object-suggestion form send your input (and, incidentally, your IP address as part of any standard web request) to a small backend we run on Cloudflare Workers, so we can tally votes and collect suggestions. We use this only to run the site's features — not for tracking or advertising.
